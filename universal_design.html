Universal Design
Universal design is designing something that is accessible to all, whether that is websites, buildings, or products. This is done by making certain design rules standard or even mandatory. These rules are there for a reason, and helps people navigate the whatever is made with it. Universal design when it comes to websites are rules to make the website more user friendly, readable, and more professional.
