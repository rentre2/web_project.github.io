Copyright
Copyright is an exclusive right that creator have over their own work for a limited time. This right is makes it so that other cannot copy or otherwise use their network without explicit permission from the owner of the work. Copyrights are meant to protect what you make, the expression of an idea but not the idea itself. Copyrighted works can on the other hand be used without permission if what you make with it goes under fair use. Fair use allows you to take parts of someone’s work and create something new with it, such as a review of a movie that contains clips of the movie.
